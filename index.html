About Me

Engineering is a way of life for me and I'm inspired to use my skills to develop mobile apps that transform the way we interact with and experience the world around us. My apps are diverse and span genres including lifestyle, social media, TV and radio broadcasting, word games, history and geography, and events.

I love combining my interests in creative ways, such as bridging mechanical engineering, medicine, and app development to build a medical prototype of an iOS-connected Ankle-Foot Orthosis, or researching machine learning using computerized artificial intelligence vision. I am addicted to mobile software engineering and seeking new ways to connect people and objects to mobile devices.

I learned business acumen and entrepreneurs' grit from my father, who trained me from childhood how to lead and grow a business from the ground up. Today, I have co-founded several tech start-ups, spanning both Africa and the United States. I also love mentoring and consulting with startups that have a need for bleeding-edge or connected technologies.
